Courses We Offer

Civil Engineering Department

This course aims at producing highly qualified, efficient technicians capable of meeting the requirements of the basic level of the Civil Engineering field. During the course of study, the student is exposed to various subjects of Civil Engineering such as Planning, Drafting (both manual and computer aided), Surveying, Estimation & Costing, Construction Technology, Basics of Highway, Railway, Bridge, Airport, Tunnel & Harbour Engineering. The course also offers adequate exposure to Reinforced Concrete Structures, Quality Control, Material Testing, Construction Management & Entrepreneurship, Professional Practice & Office Procedures. Students also get a feel of Extensive Survey, Building Drawing, Irrigation & Bridge Drawing & Structural Engineering Drawing. Students are also exposed to software like AUTOCAD & STAAD.

Computer Science Engineering Department

This course aims at producing highly qualified, efficient technicians capable of meeting the requirements of the basic level of the Information Science & Technology field. In this course diploma in computer science the students are exposed to Basics of Computers, Microprocessor, & software like C, C++, Java, VB, LINUX, Database Management Systems, Web Technology, Multimedia & the latest. NET software. In addition they are also exposed to PC Hardware, Maintenance & Networking.

Electrical And Elecronics Engneering Department

Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) covers the generation, storage and use of power. We use so many items in our daily life use power (mains, batteries, petrol/ motors or even solar panels). EEE is a very important part of our everyday life. The study of EEE can involve large amounts of power (eg power and control engineering) and large devices (eg power stations and electric vehicles) or small amounts of power (eg computer, communications and information engineering) and small devices (eg MP3 players and cell phones). The vision of this Department is to provide the leadership to enable India's excellence in the field of Electrical Engineering. The Department is committed to advancement of the frontiers of knowledge in Electrical Engineering and to provide the students with a stimulating and rewarding learning experience.

Elecronics and communication Engneering Department

Electronics is the brain of Modern Technology. Even the computer is an electronic device. It is an evergreen subject that provides opportunities for technicians in following areas of Engineering like Maintainance & Service, Research Assistance, Production Control, Quality Control and Marketing.
